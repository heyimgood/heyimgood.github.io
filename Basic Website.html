Car Wiki: Tesla Model S

Overview

The Tesla Model S is an all-electric five-door liftback sedan, produced by Tesla, Inc. It was introduced in 2012 and has since gained popularity for its impressive performance, long range, and cutting-edge technology. The Model S is one of the most recognized electric vehicles in the world.
